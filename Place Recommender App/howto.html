HOW TO USE

Using the app is incredibly simple and user-friendly. Just input your desired destination—whether it's a country or city—and select the type of place you're interested in, such as hotels, bars, restaurants, or other attractions. With just a few taps, you'll receive personalized recommendations tailored to your preferences.

STEP 1:

Select your desired country and city using our user friendly live search.

STEP 2:

Select your desired place of interest

STEP 3:

Select, navigate the map and discover new places to attend. Enjoy!
